About Banaras Silk Art-7 (Ayesha Sarees) - Pure Banarasi Silk Saree Manufacturer in Varanasi

About Business Owner

Welcome to our shop! I am a traditional saree manufacturer based in the historic city of Varanasi (Banaras). My family has been working with authentic weavers for generations to create premium quality Banarasi Sarees.

My goal is simple: to keep our traditional weaving art alive while making sure modern women get the finest, softest sarees directly from our manufacturing facility without any middlemen expenses.

About Banaras Silk Art-7

Banaras Silk Art-7 (popularly known locally and on Instagram as Ayesha Sarees, Varanasi) is a leading online factory store for 100% genuine Banarasi silk sarees.

We handle everything from saree collecting from manufacturer to final quality checking. Because we sell directly from manufacturer, we offer premium bridal sarees, handloom weaves, and party-wear sarees at the suitable wholesale prices. Whether you want a single saree for a wedding or need bulk stock for your retail shop, we deliver excellence all over India.
