Privacy Policy

Welcome to Banaras Silk Art-7(A Banarasi Saree Store). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and secure your information when you visit our website (silk-art-7.vercel.app) or make a purchase.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or attempt to make a purchase through our website, we collect certain necessary details from you, including:

  • Personal Identity: Full Name.
  • Contact Details: Phone Number.
  • Delivery Details: Complete Shipping Address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for business operations, which includes:

  • Processing your order and managing deliveries.
  • Communicating with you regarding order verification or status updates.
  • Preventing fraudulent activity and keeping our checkout store secure.

3. Data Security & Third-Party Services

Your data security is important to us. We use industry-standard security measures to ensure a safe shopping experience:

  • Database Security (Firebase): Your order records are securely stored and processed using Firebase Firestore database, protected by strict server-side security rules to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Data Protection: We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third-party marketing companies. Your details are strictly used for fulfilling your orders.

4. Data Retention

When you place an order through the website, we will maintain your order information for our internal records (such as tracking delivery and sales tax calculations) unless and until you ask us to delete this information.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us via the details provided on our official Contact Us page.
